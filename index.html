Our Story

✦
👨‍🍳 Chef Laurent in the Kitchen

A Passion Born from Tradition

Ruby was born from a simple yet profound belief: that exceptional food has the power to bring people together, spark conversation, and create lasting memories.

Founded by Chef Laurent Moreau in 2019, our bistro blends the warmth of classic European cooking with bold, contemporary flavors sourced from local farms and artisan producers within a 50-mile radius.

Every morning begins with our bakers shaping fresh sourdough, our baristas perfecting each pour, and our kitchen team hand-selecting the finest seasonal ingredients. This is not just a restaurant — it is a gathering place, a hearth around which stories unfold.
