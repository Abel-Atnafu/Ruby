The Queen's Story

👑

Where Every Burger Wears a Crown

Queen Burger was born with one royal decree: to serve burgers so good, they deserve a throne. Every patty is well-seasoned, every bun is butter-toasted, and every bite is crafted to make you feel like royalty.

We use premium ground beef, fresh provolone cheese, our legendary house sauce, and crispy extras that set us apart. From our signature Royal Cheese Burger to the legendary Queen Lava Burger — we don't do ordinary.

Located in Addis Ababa, we've become the go-to spot for burger lovers who know that great flavor is non-negotiable.
