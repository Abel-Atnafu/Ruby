Where Every Burger Wears a Crown

Queen Burger was born with one royal decree: to serve burgers so magnificent they deserve a throne. Every patty is hand-seasoned, every bun butter-toasted, every bite an act of devotion.

We use premium ground beef, fresh provolone cheese, our legendary house sauce, and crispy extras that set us apart from the ordinary. From the signature Royal Cheese to the legendary Queen Lava Burger — we don't do mediocre.

Located in the heart of Addis Ababa, we've become the destination for burger lovers who know that exceptional flavor is non-negotiable.
